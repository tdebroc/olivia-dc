Data Governance

Governance, the Olivia way: not a rulebook people fear, but a framework people actually enjoy. Below are the policies she lived by — each one promoting compliance, transparency and innovation.

Chief Data Steward
Olivia Phan
Accountable for the most valuable asset in the building: people who trust their data — and trust each other. Tenure: 5 remarkable years. Successor: irreplaceable, but inspired.

Active policies

💛

POL-001 · Care Before Compliance

Every dataset, every colleague, every guest is handled with genuine care first. “Toujours au petit soin” — the rule that makes all the others unnecessary.

enforced
🔗

POL-002 · Connect Everything (and Everyone)

No data island, no lonely colleague. Relationships are first-class entities. Build links, introduce people, and watch value compound. Orphaned records will be lovingly adopted.

enforced
💡

POL-003 · Always Ship an Idea

Never leave a meeting without at least one creative, innovative idea. Stagnation is the only forbidden state. Brainstorms run hot; the backlog is never bored.

enforced
📖

POL-004 · Democratise the Data

Knowledge belongs to everyone. Articles, videos, newsletters, Viva Engage posts and upskilling sessions keep data literacy high and jargon low. If only one person understands it, it isn’t done.

enforced
✨

POL-005 · Stay Courageous, Keep the Smile

Through every high and low, stay courageous, stay joyful, stay yourself. A sincere smile is the most trusted quality signal we have — and it never fails an audit.

immutable
👗

POL-006 · Dress the Data (and Yourself) Well

Presentation matters. Dashboards should be elegant; outfits should be Saint Laurent. A well-presented insight is a well-adopted insight. Default theme: timeless.

recommended

Governance principles

🔍

Transparency

Off-the-shelf visibility of every asset, for business and tech alike. Nothing hidden, nothing gatekept.

🧭

Traceability

Automated lineage from source-aligned products to business dashboards. Always know where it came from.

🚀

Innovation

Governance that enables, never blocks. Embedded GenAI, self-service, and a culture that says “yes, and…”.
